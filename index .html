📝 About Me

I’m Lulu B (Benedict Wambua)a creative soul driven by both code and melody. With a passion for building responsive, user-friendly websites, I aim to make every digital experience smooth and engaging.

Beyond the screen, I express myself through music—writing and performing songs that speak to the heart. Whether I’m developing a web app or composing lyrics, I’m all about turning passion into impact.

🎓 Educational Background

I attended Kithimani HGM Secondary School in Kithimani, Machakos County, where I built a strong academic foundation, especially in Computer Studies.


I later joined Borabu Technical Training Institute in Nyamira County, where I’m currently pursuing a Diploma in Computer Programming.


In my first year at Borabu, I performed excellently, consistently ranking among the top in my class.


I’m also studying at PLP Africa on a full scholarship, sharpening my digital and leadership skills to become a well-rounded tech professional.
